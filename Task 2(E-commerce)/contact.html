Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) and overnight shipping options are also available at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in original condition with tags attached. Returns are free for defective items.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track orders in your account dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and offer buy-now-pay-later options through Klarna and Afterpay.

Do you have a size guide?

Yes, each product page includes detailed size charts. We also offer free exchanges if the size doesn't fit perfectly.
